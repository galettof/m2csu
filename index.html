Macaulay2 Workshop and Conference at Cleveland State University

We are organizing a virtual Macaulay2 workshop from May 11 to May 15, 2020. The goal is to bring together researchers of different backgrounds and expertise levels to collaborate on developing packages for Macaulay2.

We are also planning a follow-up conference to be held at Cleveland State University in Spring/Summer 2021. Stay tuned!

This workshop is organized by Federico Galetto, Courtney Gibbons, Hiram López, and Branden Stone.
